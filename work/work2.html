“ACTION POTENTIAL” TAKES THE WORKFLOW I DEVELOPED IN ALPHA[BETA]FOR CONTROLLING DIGITAL SYSTEMS WITH REALTIME EEG DATA AND APPLIES IT TO A PHYSICAL DEVICE, OUTSIDE OF THE SCREEN. IT IS AN AMAZING EXPERIENCE TO BE ABLE TO DELIBERATELY MOVE AN OBJECT IN DIGITAL SPACE, AND I AM INTERESTED IN HOW MUCH MORE POWERFUL IT MIGHT BE TO CONTROL SOMETHING PHYSICAL AND “REAL”. AWARE OF FERROFLUID FROM A YOUNG AGE (I WAS ALWAYS FASCINATED BY MAGNETS AS A CHILD) I DECIDED TO BUILD A DEVICE THAT WOULD ALLOW ME TO CONTROL A GRID OF ELECTROMAGNETS, TREATING EACH MAGNET AS A PIXEL. A TANK OF FERROFLUID IS PLACED UPON THE GRID. WHEN ACTIVATED BY THE INCOMING EEG COMMANDS DELIBERATELY SENT BY PARTICIPANTS, SHAPES, MOVEMENTS AND BEHAVIOURS ARE FORMED WITHIN THE FERROFLUID.
