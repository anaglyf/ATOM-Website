ALPHA[BETA] IS A SYSTEM DESIGNED TO EXPLORE BRAIN COMPUTER INTERFACING AND ASK QUESTIONS ABOUT POST LANGUAGE COMMUNICATION. I DEVELOPED THE SYSTEM AS A RESPONSE TO AN LG DISPLAY/RCA COMPETITION TO DEMONSTRATE THE RADICAL POTENTIAL OF TRANSPARENT OLED TECHNOLOGY. DIGITAL INTERFACE TECHNOLOGY IN ITS CURRENT STATE CREATES A BOTTLENECK EFFECT AT THE EXCHANGE POINT BETWEEN HUMAN AND DIGITAL, ALPHA[BETA] EXPLORES THE BRAIN COMPUTER INTERFACE AS A POSSIBLE ROUTE TO TACKLING THIS. USING LG TRANSPARENT OLED DISPLAYS ENABLED ME TO CREATE AN EXPERIENCE DIFFERENT FROM THE CONVENTIONAL LANGUAGE OF DIGITAL INTERACTIONS, PROVIDING A PLATFORM FOR THE VISUAL ELEMENT THAT WAS DIVORCED FROM THE CLASSIC “BLACK MIRROR” DISPLAY, SUGGESTING A FUTURE WHERE THERE IS TRANSPARENCY BETWEEN HUMANS, INFORMATION AND THE PHYSICAL WORLD.
